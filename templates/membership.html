What is ACM?

Association for Computing Machinery, ACM, is organized into over 170 local chapters. ACM has events and chapters all over the world. An amazing membership for those interested in Advancing Computer Science as a Science & Profession. If you have any questions or would like to contact us for more information, please email us! css@cpp.edu

How to pay:

You can pay for membership at our general meetings (see Calendar for events). Cash or check accepted. For checks, please make them payable to Cal Poly Pomona Computer Science Society.
